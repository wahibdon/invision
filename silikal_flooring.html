Silikal Flooring Systems

Silikal® is a renewable floor designed to last the lifetime of the structure.  An enhanced MMA coating, Silikal® will give you a totally seamless floor surface that is completely pinhole and porous free in addition to being an extremely strong industrial floor coating.    And you may qualify for LEED’s credits.

 Some of the main features include:

  • Hygienic – will not harbor bacteria and molds, it is a non-porous, pinhole free seamless flooring surface
  • Cleanable – Water, dirt and bacteria rest on the surface due to is non-porous nature
  • Safe & Slip Resistant – Anti-slip flooring surface that prevents or reduces slips, fall and flooring slip accidents
  • Impact Resistant & Durable - Silikal® withstands mechanical abrasion forklifts and chemical attack, designed to hold up to the harshest conditions
  • Looks & Appearance – Many flooring colors & flooring designs are available for wet, cold & hot conditions or in combination
  • Long Life & Renewable - Silikal® flooring will provide years of service with limited maintenance, lower life cycle cost
  • Easy to Repair - Silikal® bonds chemically to itself without sanding or grinding. Repairs go quick and painless
  • Low Temperature Curing – Both our commercial flooring and industrial floors can be installed down to Zero Degrees 0’F – Which means no down time for your business

Silikal® flooring systems can be used in a wide variety of commercial and industrial businesses including:

  • Restaurants
  • Veterinary Clinics
  • Food Production
  • Schools & Education
  • Offices
  • Commercial Kitchens
  • Warehouses
  • Hospitals
  • Laboratories
  • Retail & Commercial
