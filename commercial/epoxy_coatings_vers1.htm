Epoxy Flooring

At InVision Flooring Systems Slik Crete, LLC. epoxy floor coatings are high performance epoxy systems that have been designed to provide a high gloss, semi-gloss or stipple finish with better chemical resistant protection. Our various epoxy options offer protection against splash and spillage of many process chemicals, caustic cleaners, oils, fuels and acids. Epoxy floor coatings provide attractive finishes and should be used wherever a high-build, protective coating is specified. Epoxy coatings are applied in warehouses, industrial plants and various commercial facilities because of their chemical and abrasion resistant properties, ability to hide imperfections and they meet or exceed USDA, FDA and OSHA inspection requirements.
